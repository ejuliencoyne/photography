About Me

Hi! I'm Julien. Maine photographer. I take photos of events, weddings, activities, items, places, people. I'm a pretty no-frills person who doesn't do much posing or planning. I use a lot of film. I don't take very many pictures at a given shoot. (Quality over quantity!)

My photography availability is very limited, but please reach out anyway if you're interested in working together. I'm especially interested in small informal weddings, sports/outdoors, and music.

2024 statement: If a project or event seems like a good fit, there's a good chance I will do it for free/at cost! So what's the harm in asking?
